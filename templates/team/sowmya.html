Dr Sowmya Bommasani

MBBS FRANZCP

About Me :

Dr Sowmya Bommasani is a Consultant Psychiatrist specialising in areas of general adult psychiatry and women’s mental health. Additionally, her areas of expertise include the management of substance use disorders, including detoxification. She is also currently in the process of completing advanced training in addiction medicine with the College of Physicians.

She has many years of experience providing inpatient and outpatient care in assessing and managing a wide range of conditions, both in the public and private healthcare sectors. Dr Bommasani takes a personalised approach in assessing the needs of her patients and develops tailor-made treatment and management plans through thorough assessments and consultations.


Areas Of Expertise
  • Anxiety disorders
  • Obsessive-compulsive and related disorders
  • Mood disorders
  • Personality disorders
  • Psychotic disorders
  • Women’s mental health
  • Substance Use
