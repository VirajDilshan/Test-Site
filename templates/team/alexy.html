Dr Alexey Sidorov

MB CHB MRCPSYCH. FRANZCP

About Me :

Dr Alexey Sidorov is a Consultant Forensic and General Psychiatrist in both public sector and in the private sector (in medicolegal and general practice). He has varied experience in preparation of reports for Courts and Tribunals, in a variety of jurisdictions, both criminal and civil. He has experience in being involved in a forensic psychiatry clinical trial, as well as working in a variety of settings, including the High Secure Forensic Hospital, Long Bay Prison Hospital, Court Liaison Service, medium secure unit, prison clinics, general hospitals, and private practice.

Dr Sidorov takes a personalised approach in assessing the needs of his patients and develops tailor-made treatment and management plans through assessments and consultations.
