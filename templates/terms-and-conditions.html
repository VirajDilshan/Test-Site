8. Appointments, Confirmations and Cancellations

If a gap charge is applicable, payment in full is required to confirm an initial individual consultation at the time the appointment is made. Subsequent appointments also require payment in full at the time of booking.

In order to proceed with any appointment for which there is an out of pocket cost, you will need to pay a processing fee of $0.50, through the Epsychiatry client portal. Here you can select the psychiatrist or psychologist you would like to see, select an appointment time, review the appointment, enter your credit card details, then click “Book Now”.

If you are eligible for Medicare rebates for Telehealth services you are required to have a referral letter from your GP. We would need a copy of the GP referral letter before your first appointment.

If you are required to pay out of pocket costs, your credit card details will be taken at the time of booking. A gap fee amount will after the appointment be charged to this card.Your Medicare rebate will then be processed automatically. If for any reason Medicare finds you are not eligible for a rebate your credit card will be billed the full amount for the appointment. Additional costs will be incurred in line with the consultation fees of different clinicians.

If you are eligible and would like to Bulk Bill for your appointment you are required to have a referral from your GP. If you are Bulk Billed, you will not be charged for your appointment.

If you are accessing Epsychiatry using Medicare rebates you will need to have a GP review before your 7th appointment for sessions with a psychologist; or within 12 months of the last referral if your sessions are with a psychiatrist.

Both the client and Epsychiatry have the right to terminate any services agreement for any reason, including the ending of the services that are already underway. However, no refunds if there are out of pocket costs shall be offered where an appointment is deemed to have begun and is, for all intents and purposes, underway.

If you have booked an appointment and want to reschedule you must inform Epsychiatry 48 hours before the appointment.

We reserve the right to update our cancellation policies to best suit the requirements of Epsychiatry and clinicians working with Epsychiatry.

If technical difficulties occur, please report these to Epsychiatry as soon as possible so we are able to discuss these with you and ensure a fair outcome.

If for any reason your clinician is unable to attend the appointment, we will notify you by email and you will be contacted to reschedule the appointment.
