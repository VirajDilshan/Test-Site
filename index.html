Why choose us

We give care and attention to offer you online mental health support right across the borders of Australia. Easily accessible “right from the comfort of my couch”, “with a cuppa in hand” are some ways in which our customers like to connect with us. The proverbial line outside the door is steadily growing thanks to our short waitlists. Our super-efficient and friendly staff lend you a virtual handhold guiding you through how it all works, up to the point of seeing you through to a clinician who will take on your care. We help you understand the cause of your distress, recognise your symptoms, and identify treatments that can help you. Join us and see how it brings about positive change in you

Tempted yet hesitant to reach out?

It is human nature to worry and doubt. This serves as a protective instinct at times in our lives. If there is one thing we’re learnt over the years, it is that everyone is different. We all have different needs and wants. Finding the right fit for you is key. From your very first contact with us, our highly trained and knowledgeable support staff work diligently to fully understand your needs and match you with the ‘best fit’ clinician. We don’t stop there either. With your help, our carefully developed appointment notification and reminder systems work to keep you gently informed yet not overloaded with messages. Life can get busy, pulling you in all directions. Let us work with you to take some of that burdens off of your shoulders.
